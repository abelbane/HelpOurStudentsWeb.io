Reports.

The President’s Report is usually distributed twice each year - in January and in June/July. It contains an update on recent activities and notice of meetings. Every President’s Report includes a financial statement and excerpts from the letters from the recipients describing how the Help Our Students Program has impacted their lives. The letters are edited to remove any reference that might identify the student.
